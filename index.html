Ultra Ordem is a browser-based multiplayer card game platform I created to address the inconvenience of forgetting physical cards during travel. 3D Bin Packing Algorithm for Express Delivery

I conducted research on the three-dimensional bin packing problem for express delivery logistics under the supervision of a professor at Fudan University. Using Satisfiability Modulo Theories (SMT), I developed an algorithm that achieved a 5% efficiency improvement over existing heuristic methods. This work deepened my understanding of constraint solving and optimization in practical applications.
